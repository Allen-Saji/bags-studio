# BagsStudio

BagsStudio is the post-launch creator studio for Bags coins.

It helps creators identify real supporters, run momentum campaigns, and reward the wallets that keep a coin alive after launch.

## Core Thesis
Most products help creators launch a coin.

**BagsStudio helps them build what comes after.**

## Why It Exists
After launch, many coins lose momentum because creators lack:
- supporter visibility
- loyalty tooling
- structured community campaigns
- post-launch operating systems

BagsStudio solves this by giving creators a dedicated studio for post-launch growth, supporter engagement, and momentum retention.

## Core Product Areas
- **Studio Dashboard** — coin overview and momentum health
- **Supporter Map** — understand who is really backing the coin
- **Conviction Score** — identify loyal and high-signal supporters
- **Campaigns** — run momentum activations for the community
- **Reward Sets** — reward wallets helping sustain the coin

## Core Product Flow
1. Creator selects a Bags coin
2. BagsStudio ingests Bags-native data
3. Supporter conviction and ranking are computed
4. Creator launches a campaign
5. Supporters connect wallets and view eligibility
6. Creator reviews and rewards top contributors

## Product Promise
**Turn launch hype into lasting community momentum.**
