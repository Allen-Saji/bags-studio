# BagsStudio Brand Kit v1

## Name
**BagsStudio**

## Core Positioning
BagsStudio is the post-launch creator studio for Bags coins.

It helps creators sustain momentum after launch by identifying real supporters, running campaigns, and rewarding the wallets that keep a coin alive.

## Brand Thesis
Launch is a moment.
Community is what lasts.

BagsStudio exists for everything that happens after the coin goes live.

## Tagline Options
### Primary
**Turn launch hype into lasting community momentum**

### Secondary
- **The creator studio for post-launch growth**
- **Build the community after the coin goes live**
- **Reward supporters. Sustain momentum.**
- **Everything after launch starts here**

## Messaging Pillars
### 1. Post-launch is the real game
Most products help creators launch a coin. BagsStudio helps them build what comes after.

### 2. Supporters matter
Not all wallets are equal. BagsStudio helps creators identify and reward the people actually sustaining momentum.

### 3. Creators need tools, not hope
After launch, creators need visibility, campaigns, loyalty loops, and community tooling—not just tweets and vibes.

## Tone
- sharp
- calm
- creator-first
- modern
- premium, not corporate
- onchain-native without sounding degen

## Voice Rules
### Do
- speak clearly
- emphasize creators and supporters
- make post-launch momentum the central story
- use short, strong phrases

### Don't
- sound like generic SaaS copy
- use exaggerated crypto hype language
- rely on tokenomics buzzword soup
- overuse meme language

## Visual Direction
### Overall Feel
A dark, premium creator control room for live coins and active communities.

### Aesthetic Keywords
- control room
- studio
- momentum
- loyalty
- live signal
- post-launch energy
- premium dark mode

### Avoid
- exchange terminal clones
- memecoin casino aesthetics
- corporate BI dashboard vibes
- cartoonish token graphics

- bold geometric headlines
- clean sans-serif body
- mono or semi-mono for data, ranks, and scores
