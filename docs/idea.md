# Momentum OS - Idea Notes

## Problem
Coins often die after launch because there is no retention engine for creators and no reason for supporters to keep showing up beyond price action.

## Goal
Create a post-launch operating layer that helps Bags coins sustain audience, community, and momentum.

## Audience
### Primary
- creators / developers launching Bags coins

### Secondary
- traders / early supporters / community members

## Product Principles
- Bags-native, not generic
- useful immediately after launch
- helps both sides of the market
- encourages repeat participation
- creates measurable community momentum

## Strongest Product Wedge
Supporter Score + Momentum Campaigns

This gives creators a way to identify and reward real supporters while giving supporters a reason to remain active.

## Why It Can Stand Out
- solves a real post-launch problem
- aligned with Bags judging criteria
- can demonstrate both app utility and onchain relevance
- has startup potential beyond hackathon framing
